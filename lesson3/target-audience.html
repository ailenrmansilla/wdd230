Target Audience:

Adults of any gender who are bussiness owners, vendors, entrepenuers, poeple with experience in commerce or people who are starting to participate in that field, people who want training in commerce, and people who want to make their bussiness grow.

Personas

Persona 1: Lucy is a 29 years old woman entrepeuner looking for financial aid to open a second store of her clothes brand. Persona 2: George is a 45 years old electronic's company owner. He wants to find a bussiness partner to provide him with tools.

Scenarios:

  • Poeple who want to receive updated information of the local commerce look on our website.
  • Entrepenuers come to our website to find useful resources to apply on their bussiness.
  • A business owner wants to join our chamber to receive financial aid and other benefits.
  • A company owner wants to find a business partener to increase their reach.
  • A new bussiness wants to join a chamber of commerce to expand its brand abroad.
