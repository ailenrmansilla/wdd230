Rosario City, Argentina

The city of Rosario, in the Santa Fe province, counts with more than 1.5 million of people. It's placed next to the Parana river.

Between its many attractions, we find the "Monument to the flag", represented by the building of a ship bringing the flame of liberty and the flag of Argentina in it.

A distinctive view of the city are the strees full of palms and other trees.

The city is in front of the river and the islands of the neighbor province, and surrounded by the soya fields.
People favorite place always will be the shore walk. There you can see the beautiful of the city and feel the peace of the river.
