Wedding Reception Hall

Our beautiful wedding reception hall can be easily customized with the couple's chosen decoration. We provide a simple catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and serving.

Food Setup Options

Option 1: Hors d'oeuvre appetizer

Small bits of appetizing food, as spicy meat, fish, or cheese, served on crackers or small pieces of toast, and little sandwiches.

Option 2: Vegan menu

A full vegan catering option including appetizer and main course.

Option 3: Sweet catering

We just provide a sweet catering and a simple cute wedding cake and you have kitchen access for food preparation and serving.
